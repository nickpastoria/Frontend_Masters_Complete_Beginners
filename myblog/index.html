However, this knowledge did not help me get more done. Which I guess is the issue with learning you have a disability. Most of the work you have to do as the person with the disability is understanding what your limits are and being kinder to yourself. ADHD is a fortunate disability to have because the medication for it is very efficable. For me it feels like none of the options have been all that helpful.
